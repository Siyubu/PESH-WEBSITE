About Us

PESH is a youth-led initiative committed to empowering people in Rwanda through digital access, skills training, and community support. We believe that everyone, especially the younger generation, deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed in today’s digital world. Through our programs, we provide access to technology, mentorship, and practical skills with a hope to open doors to education and employment.

Our Programs

Digital Training

We provide hands-on training in computer skills, internet use, and digital tools to prepare youth for real-world opportunities.

  • Tue & Thu: 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM (Youth)
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM (Children)

Device Support

Access to a personal computer can significantly enhance learning and productivity. For those without one, we provide computers at our hub where individuals can come, use them for learning, and return them after use.

Mentorship

We mentor young women, many of whom go on to volunteer at our hub. They play a key role in teaching children during Saturday sessions in the school term, and we extend these sessions by adding two additional days during school holidays.

Community Outreach

We collaborate with local government to engage community groups and promote digital awareness. Through our outreach programs, we help individuals and communities understand and use technology to improve their daily lives.
